	When the app is first opened, click on "login rider" or "login driver", A new activity shows, and please allow the app use your location, then there is a little button on the top right, click that button and you can your current location(that little button might not show up at first time using this app, but it will show up at second time). For "login rider", when user is searching a place, please input the full name of the place, such as "West Edmonton Mall","Southgate Centre","Edmonton International Airport",otherwise, app will go back to "where to go" page and user will input new location.
	After running the app too many times on an emulator, the emulator will be full and not work well, please use a new emulator.
